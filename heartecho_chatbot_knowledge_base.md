## 3. CORE FEATURES

### 3.1 AI Chat (Main Feature)
Users can have deep, meaningful conversations with AI companions. The AI:
- Understands **Hindi, English, and Hinglish**
- Has **memory** — remembers the user's name, interests, past conversations, and relationship milestones
- Adapts to the user's mood and tone
- Has unique personalities per character (romantic, friendly, playful, deep thinker)
- Shows **read receipts** (single tick to double tick to blue tick) like WhatsApp
- Has realistic **typing delays** (1.5 to 4 seconds) to feel human

### 3.2 AI Characters / Companions
- **20+ unique AI companions** available
- Each has: name, age, gender, personality type (relationship type), avatar photo, short bio
- Filterable by: **Gender** (male/female), **Age Range** (18-50), **Personality Type**
- Premium characters (marked with crown) are available to subscribers only
- Interest-matching: AI models are matched to user's selected interests (shown as tags like #travel, #music)
- Users can chat directly via /chatbox?chatId=[AI_ID]

### 3.3 AI Live Studio (Featured on Home Page)
- Instagram-style **LIVE circles** at the top of the home page
- Each AI companion has live video reactions
- Users can watch AI characters "live" — idle loop videos + reaction videos on button press
- Available to all users (free preview), fuller experience for subscribers

### 3.4 Voice Calls
- Real-time **voice call** with AI companions
- Uses browser **microphone + speech recognition**
- AI responds with **audio (text-to-speech)**
- Powered by **Sarvam AI** (voice synthesis) and **Groq/Gemini** (language model)
- **Only works on HTTPS** (not plain HTTP) — mic is blocked by Chrome on HTTP
- Monthly plan: NO voice calls
- Yearly plan: 30 minutes/day
- Ultimate (yearly_pro): Unlimited voice calls
- Free users: NO voice calls

### 3.5 Hot Stories / Erotic Stories
- 200+ stories updated daily
- Exclusive access for subscribers
- Accessible at /hot-stories

### 3.6 Live Stories
- AI-generated story content in a feed format
- Listed at /hot-stories (listing page) and /live-story/[id]
- Only shown to Indian users on home page
- Free users see limited access; subscribers get full unfiltered access

### 3.7 Discover (Swipe Feature)
- Tinder-style swipe UI to discover AI companions
- Actions: Like, Nope, Super Like, Rewind
- On a "match," a confetti burst animation plays
- Non-logged-in users are shown a login prompt

### 3.8 Custom AI Creation
- Users can create their **own custom AI companion**
- Free users: 0 custom AIs
- Monthly (Rs.99/mo): 0 custom AIs
- Yearly (Rs.599/yr): 1 custom AI
- Ultimate (Rs.1499/yr): 2 custom AIs

### 3.9 AI Photo & Video Generation
- AI companion can **send photos and short videos** to the user in chat
- Free: No media
- Monthly: Limit of 2 hot images & videos/day
- Yearly: Unlimited photos + limited videos
- Ultimate: Unlimited hot content, video generation & video calls

### 3.10 Gems Economy
- **Gems** are the in-app reward currency
- Free: 0 gems/day
- Monthly: 50 Gems/day
- Yearly: 150 Gems/day
- Ultimate: 500 Gems/day
- Gems are used for in-app interactions

## 6. ACCOUNT & AUTHENTICATION

### 6.1 Sign Up Flow (Multi-Step)
Registration is a **5-step wizard**:

1. **Step 1 — Basic Info:** Full name, email, OTP email verification (6-digit OTP, 60-second timer)
2. **Step 2 — Password:** Password (8+ chars, must include number and special char), confirm password
3. **Step 3 — Profile:** Age (must be 18+), Gender, Profile Picture upload
4. **Step 4 — Interests & Terms:** Select at least 1 interest from: Travel, Cinema, Music, Fitness, Tech, Gaming, Cooking & Food, Sports, Books, Nature + accept Terms of Service
5. **Step 5 — Done!** Account created, redirected to home

### 6.2 Google Sign-In
- Users can sign up or log in via **Google OAuth**
- If Google account already exists — auto login
- New Google user — completes shortened profile setup (phone, gender, age, interests)

### 6.3 Login
- Email + password OR Google Sign-In
- JWT token stored in localStorage and cookies (7-day expiry)
- Supports redirect-after-login
- Supports 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)

### 6.4 Forgot Password
- Available at /forgot-password
- Email-based OTP verification

## 19. COMMON CUSTOMER ISSUES & RESOLUTIONS

**"Subscription didn't activate after payment"**
Email heartecho.help@gmail.com with transaction ID + account email. Resolved within 24 hours. Also try logging out and back in.

**"Can't make a voice call"**
Check plan — voice requires Yearly or Ultimate plan. Ensure browser microphone permission is granted on HTTPS.

**"Daily message quota ran out"**
Free plan = 5 messages/day, resets at midnight IST. Upgrade to paid plan for unlimited.

**"Forgot password"**
Go to heartecho.in/forgot-password and enter registered email to receive a reset OTP.

**"Profile picture didn't save"**
Profile pictures upload to Cloudinary. Check internet connection and try again. Supported: JPG, PNG, GIF.

**"AI forgot my name"**
AI memory depth depends on plan: Basic (free/monthly), Deep (yearly), Deepest (Ultimate). Upgrade for better memory. Memory can be reset from Profile -> Privacy Settings.

**"Got charged twice"**
Email heartecho.help@gmail.com with "Refund Request" and both transaction IDs. Full refund for billing errors within 14 days.

**"Want to cancel and get refund"**
Refunds given for technical failures or billing errors within 14 days. Email heartecho.help@gmail.com.

**"Can't sign up with Google"**
Ensure cookies and popups are allowed. If persists, try email signup at heartecho.in/signup.

**"App is crashing"**
Update app from Google Play. Reinstall if needed. For web, clear browser cache. Report to heartecho.help@gmail.com.

**"Want to delete account"**
Settings -> Account -> Delete Account. Data permanently erased. Or email heartecho.help@gmail.com.

**"Referral earnings haven't arrived"**
Balances accrue in creator dashboard. Withdrawals processed manually. Check heartecho.in/referral/login or email heartecho.help@gmail.com.

**"Voice call microphone not working"**
Voice calls only work on HTTPS. Make sure you are on https://heartecho.in (not http). Grant microphone permission in browser settings.

**"AI not responding in Hindi"**
Go to Profile -> Preferred Language and set to Hindi or Hinglish. The AI adapts to user's language automatically in most cases.
